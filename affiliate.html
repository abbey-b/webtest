ibd Business Advice Group

Pennine Consultancy is an accredited business advisor in the ibd Business Advice Group and is part of the 200 specialist business advisers across the UK, ibd Business Advice has developed a ‘bank’ of specialists that all businesses, from SMEs to large corporations, can call on for advice and guidance. The same business advisers can also work ‘hands on’ with management teams to implement solutions. ibd advisers come from a variety of backgrounds, from smaller companies to multinationals, but all have board-level experience in the UK.

The Federation of Small Businesses

Pennine Consultancy are members of the Federation of Small Businesses (FSB). The FSB help smaller businesses achieve their ambitions. They offer members a wide range of vital business services including advice, financial expertise, support, training and have a powerful voice which is heard in government.

GC Business Growth Hub

Pennine Consultancy have worked closely with the GC Business Growth Hub, accessing their support and helping to grow our business. The GC Business Growth Hub is part of the Growth Company who together support businesses at all stages of their growth journey with a broad range of services. GM Chamber of Commerce (GMCC)

Pennine Consultancy are members of the Greater Manchester Chamber of Commerce. The GM Chambers provide first-class business support to companies of all shapes and sizes. With over 4500 members and the largest accredited Chamber of Commerce in the UK, the GM Chambers is located in the heart of Manchester City Centre and has a strong presence within the City's ten local authority areas.

GM Chamber of Commerce website

Hearing Ambassadors

Pennine are proud to be working with a leading hearing charity, helping them promote technology that can help many individuals who are living with hearing loss. Hearing Ambassadors aims to help people with good hearing become aware of the unseen effects of hearing loss so that they understand what it is like and know how to relate to those living with hearing loss.
