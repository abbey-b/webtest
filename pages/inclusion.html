Digital Accessibility Audit

Audit all digital content (brochures, websites, etc) to determine if they are accessible to all.

Outcomes:

  • Comprehensive report with suggestions on how to deliver digital accessible that adds value to the business

Suitable for CEOs, Commercial Directors and Marketing Directors.

Delivered as a full-day assessment on-site or remotely.

Digitial Accessibility Training (Copywriters)

Understand why accessible content is so important and why it adds value.

Outcomes:

  • How to create accessible content

  • How to choose correct fonts, colours and images for accessibility

Suitable for CEOs, Sales and Marketing Directors, and Copywriters.

Delivered as 1-hour workshop either on-site or remotely.
