Our Story

Hello, I'm David!


Drawing on over 30-years experience and credibility, David is able to generate sustainable product and business improvements, with a natural ability to engage at all levels throughout any SME electro-mechanical machinery manufacturer.

Back in 2016, David suddenly lost all his hearing so has no natural hearing, however he was fast-tracked for a cochlear implant that has brought him back into the world of sound. His deafness and struggles to overcome these new barriers has led him to be at the forefront of accessible communication and inclusiveness.
